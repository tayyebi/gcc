Polybutadiene Rubber (BR) is a synthetic rubber polymerized from 1,3-butadiene. It accounts for ~28% of global synthetic rubber consumption, second only to SBR. About 70% is used in tire manufacturing and 25% in plastics modification.

### Types by Catalyst

| Lithium | 10-30 | 20-60 | 10-70 |

### Key Properties
- **High elasticity** and rebound resilience
- **Low hysteresis** — minimal heat buildup under flex
- **Excellent abrasion resistance** — outperforms most rubbers
- **Cold flexibility** — retains properties at low temperatures

### Main Applications
- **Tires** — sidewalls and tread compounds for trucks; improves wear and reduces rolling resistance
- **Plastics** — impact modifier for HIPS and ABS (4–12% loading)
- **Industrial goods** — conveyor belts, hoses, shoe soles, golf ball cores
