## Product Overview

Bitumen 60/70 is a **penetration grade paving bitumen** produced from the vacuum distillation of crude oil. It is one of the most commonly used grades worldwide due to its **balanced hardness, high adhesion, and excellent performance in moderate to warm climates**.

This grade provides optimal workability during mixing and laying, while maintaining durability and resistance under traffic load.

## What Does 60/70 Mean?

The designation **60/70** refers to the **penetration value** of the bitumen:
- Measured using a standard needle
- At **25°C**
- Under a load of **100 g for 5 seconds**
- Penetration range: **60–70 dmm (0.1 mm)**

Lower penetration = harder bitumen  
Higher penetration = softer bitumen  

Bitumen 60/70 offers a **medium hardness**, ideal for paving applications.

## Key Advantages

- High bonding strength with aggregates  
- Excellent resistance to deformation and rutting  
- Stable performance under heavy traffic  
- Good waterproofing properties  
- Proven long-term durability  
- Globally accepted paving grade
