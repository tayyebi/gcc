High-purity Refined Sulfur recovered from efficient desulfurization of Natural Gas and Crude Oil. It appears as bright yellow granules or prills and is a fundamental raw material for the chemical and agricultural industries.

### Major Applications
*   **Sulfuric Acid Production**: The precursor for the world's most widely used industrial chemical.
*   **Fertilizers**: Key ingredient in phosphates (DAP/MAP) and sulfates to improve soil quality.
*   **Rubber Manufacturing**: Essential vulcanizing agent for converting raw rubber into durable materials.
*   **Chemical Synthesis**: Used in manufacturing caprolactam, detergents, dyes, and fungicides.
