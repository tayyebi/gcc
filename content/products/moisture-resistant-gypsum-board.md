## Moisture-Resistant Gypsum Board (Green Board)

A high-performance gypsum board specifically engineered for humid and wet environments. The core is reinforced with water-repellent additives and encased in moisture-resistant green liner paper, drastically reducing water absorption and preventing mold growth.

### Technical Performance
*   **Water Resistance**: Total water absorption of less than 5% according to EN 520.
*   **Structural Integrity**: Maintains strength even in damp conditions.
*   **Base for Tiling**: Excellent substrate for ceramic tiles in wet areas (up to 32kg/m²).

### Recommended Applications
*   **Residential**: Bathrooms, shower enclosures, kitchens, and laundry rooms.
*   **Commercial**: Restrooms, locker rooms, and hospital scrub areas.
*   **Semi-exposed**: Soffits and canopies (protected from direct rain).
