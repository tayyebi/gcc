## Petroleum Naphtha

Petroleum Naphtha is a flammable liquid hydrocarbon mixture. It is a key intermediate in the oil refining process and serves as a primary feedstock for the petrochemical industry. We offer both Light Naphtha (paraffin-rich) and Heavy Naphtha (naphthene/aromatic-rich) to suit various industrial requirements.

### Key Applications

*   **Petrochemical Feedstock**: Steam cracking to produce ethylene, propylene, and other olefins.
*   **Reforming**: Catalytic reforming to produce high-octane gasoline components and aromatics (benzene, toluene, xylene).
*   **Solvents**: Used in paints, cleaning fluids, and industrial extraction processes.
*   **Fuel Blending**: As a component in various fuel formulations.

### Technical Specifications

We ensure tight quality control on critical parameters:

*   **Paraffin/Naphthene/Aromatic (PNA) Analysis**: Tailored for cracker yield optimization.
*   **Sulfur Content**: Strictly controlled to prevent catalyst poisoning in downstream units.
*   **Distillation Range**: Precise cuts (Light vs. Full Range Naphtha) defined by Initial Boiling Point (IBP) and Final Boiling Point (FBP).
