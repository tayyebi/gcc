## Industrial Salt (Sodium Chloride)

High-grade Industrial Salt (NaCl) produced through solar evaporation or rock salt mining. It is processed to achieve high chemical purity, making it suitable for demanding industrial processes, specifically Chlor-Alkali production.

### Key Applications
*   **Chlor-Alkali Industry**: Electrolysis feedstock for producing Chlorine, Caustic Soda, and Soda Ash.
*   **Water Treatment**: Regeneration of ion-exchange resins for water softening.
*   **De-icing**: Lowering freezing point of water on roads (Road Salt).
*   **Textile Dyeing**: Exhaustion agent to promote dye absorption.
