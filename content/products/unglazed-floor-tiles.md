Technical ceramic tiles designed for the most demanding environments. With no glaze to wear off, the surface texture is integral to the tile body, providing enduring slip resistance and durability even under constant heavy footfall or industrial machinery.

### Key Benefits
*   **Superior Grip**: High coefficient of friction for wet and oily areas.
*   **Wear Resistance**: Through-body color ensures scratches remain invisible.
*   **Thermal Shock Resistance**: Suitable for industrial temperature fluctuations.

### Applications
*   **Industrial**: Food processing plants, workshops, and factories.
*   **Public Spaces**: Train stations, supermarkets, and schools.
*   **Outdoor**: Terraces, pool decks, and walkways.
