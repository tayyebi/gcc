## Fire-Resistant Gypsum Board (Type X)

Professional-grade fire-resistant gypsum board designed to provide superior fire protection in commercial and residential buildings.

- **Type**: Type X (Fire-Resistant)
- **Thickness**: 5/8" (15.9mm)
- **Size**: 4' x 8', 4' x 10', 4' x 12'
- **Fire Rating**: Up to 1 hour
- **Edge Type**: Tapered

### Key Features

- Enhanced fire resistance with glass fibers
- Mold and moisture resistant core
- Easy to cut and install

- Environmentally friendly manufacturing

### Applications

- Fire-rated wall assemblies
- Ceiling applications
- Commercial buildings
- Multi-family residential
- Corridor and stairwell protection

Compatible with standard drywall installation methods. For fire-rated assemblies, follow approved UL designs.
