## Polybutadiene Rubber (PBR)

Polybutadiene Rubber (PBR) is a synthetic rubber that offers exceptional elasticity, high abrasion resistance, and low rolling resistance. It is a polymer formed from the polymerization of the monomer 1,3-butadiene. Our High-Cis PBR is particularly valued in the tire industry for improving fuel efficiency and durability.

### Key Applications

*   **Tire Manufacturing**: The primary use case, blending with natural rubber to improve tread wear and reduce heat build-up.
*   **Impact Modification**: Used as an impact modifier in High-Impact Polystyrene (HIPS) and ABS plastics.
*   **Industrial Goods**: Manufacturing of conveyor belts, hoses, shoe soles, and golf balls due to its high resilience.

### Technical Specifications

Our PBR typically features high cis-1,4 content (typically >96-97%), ensuring superior performance characteristics:

*   **High Elasticity**: Provides excellent rebound properties.
*   **Low Hysteresis**: Minimizes energy loss and heat generation during deformation.
*   **Abrasion Resistance**: Outperforms many other rubbers in wear resistance.
*   **Cold Flexibility**: Retains properties even at low temperatures.
