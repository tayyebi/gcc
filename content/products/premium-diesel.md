Our Premium Diesel is a high-performance, ultra-low sulfur diesel (ULSD) fuel engineered to meet the stringent demands of modern diesel engines. It ensures cleaner combustion, reduced emissions, and improved fuel economy.

### Key Features
*   **High Cetane Number**: Ensures rapid ingestion and smooth combustion.
*   **Low Sulfur**: Protects exhaust after-treatment systems and reduces environmental impact.
*   **Cold Weather Performance**: Additized to prevent gelling in low temperatures.

### Typical Applications
*   **Automotive**: Passenger cars, trucks, and buses complying with Euro 5/6 standards.
*   **Industrial**: Generators, off-road heavy machinery, and agricultural equipment.
*   **Marine**: High-speed diesel engines.
