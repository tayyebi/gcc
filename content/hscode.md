## HS Code Directory

The Harmonized System (HS) Code is an internationally standardized system of names and numbers to classify traded products. Use our directory to find the correct HS codes for your products.

### About HS Codes

The HS Code is maintained by the World Customs Organization (WCO) and is used by more than 200 countries and economies as a basis for:

- Customs tariffs
- International trade statistics
- Rules of origin
- Trade policies
- Monitoring of controlled goods

### Common HS Codes for Our Categories

#### Ceramics (Chapter 69)

- **6907**: Ceramic flags and paving, hearth or wall tiles
- **6908**: Glazed ceramic flags and paving
- **6910**: Ceramic sinks, wash basins, and baths
- **6914**: Other ceramic articles

#### Gypsum (Chapter 25 & 68)

- **2520**: Gypsum; anhydrite; plasters
- **6809**: Articles of plaster or compositions based on plaster

#### Petroleum (Chapter 27)

- **2709**: Petroleum oils and oils obtained from bituminous minerals, crude
- **2710**: Petroleum oils and oils obtained from bituminous minerals (other than crude)
- **2711**: Petroleum gases and other gaseous hydrocarbons
- **2712**: Petroleum jelly, paraffin wax, and similar products
